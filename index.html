ABOUT THE COMPANY

Key features of our company

Travel Nurse Staffing

Clover Health Services is a dynamic provider of travel nurse staffing services. Our Corporate staffs are the finest in the industry and are committed to paying attention to details when it comes to client requirements.

Who we are?

The demand for travel nurses is picking up as many hospitals are having a tough time providing quality patient care with limited resources. On the other end, we have experienced nurses with a passion to provide superior care.

Why are we different?

We know first-hand about the desperations of nurse-cancelled shifts or the complications due to shortage of resources that hospitals face. This understanding has enabled us to develop an integrated system that ensures a holistic healthcare experience for facilities.

Our Approach

Account Managers acquire job orders from Hospital. Our recruiters find appropriate nurses according to specialty and placements are done aligned with the client jobs at facilities across the nation.
